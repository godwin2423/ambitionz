1861 – Council act

1909 – Council act – also knowna s Morley – Minto reforms (Morley – secretary of states, Minto - Viceroy)

It introduce communal reservation in India for Muslim religion. Lord Minto is knows as the father of Indian communalism. The act also nominate one person in to viceroy’s executive council – Satyendra Prasad Sinha.

1919 – Government of India act – also known as Montague – Chelms ford reform (Montague – secretary of state, Chelmsford - viceroy)

Reservation extended to Sikh, Christian and Anglo – Indians It introduce central public service commission, bicameralism and direct election (adult suffrage). It provided for the appointment of statutory commission after 10 years. Simon commission comes in this act.
