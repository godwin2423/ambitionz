4. Ayya Vaikundar – 1809-1851

Born in Nadar samudaya. Born & died in - Swamithoppu, KAnyakumari.

Up to 21 years old his names were – Mudisooda perummal, Muthukutty. 1836 – Samasthwa samaj – first social organization in kerala. “One caste, one religion, one god & one world” - 1837. 1849 – First mirror consecration in South India – Swamithoppu. Channar revolt, upper cloth revolt – 1859. Philosophy – Ayya vazhikal(Path of father). Temples – Nizhal Thankal. Aim – Dharmayugam. A complete God – Vaikundaswamikal.

Books – Akilathiruttu, Arul nool.

5. Chattampi Swamikal

Born in – 1853 Aug 25- Kannanmoola- Kollur veedu.

Died – 1924 May 05. Monument – Panmana, Kollam – in Balabhattara temple. He never wear Kashaya vasthram. But in 1918, when went to Sri Lanka, he wear Kashaya vasthram. Chattampi – meaning leader. Philanthropist(manushya snehi) – Chattampi Swamikal. He promoted widow marriage, Opposed idol worship and stopped child marriage to old age people.

Childhood name – Ayyappan.
Youth – Kunjan pilla.
Saint – Shanmuga Das.
Ettara Yogam given the name – Vidyathi Raja.

Books:

1. Prachina Malayalam.
2. Adi Bhasha.
3. Cristumatha Chethanam.
4. Moksha Pratheepa Khandanam.
5. Advaitha Chintha Padvathi.
6. Vedathikara Niroopanam.
7. Vedantha Saram.
8. Vigraharathana Khandanam.

1853 Aug 25, Kollur, Kannammoola, tvm.
Parents – Vasudeva sarma & Nangamma.
Childhood name – Ayyappan.
Pet name – Kunjan pillai.
Earned the name Chattampi from a traditional school run by Pettayil Raman Pillai Asan. He also known as Sarva Vidyathi Raja, Kerala Yogeswaran, Shanmuga Dasan, Bala bhattarajka.
He followed Nirvrithi margam – means sanyasam. He got enlightment at Vadiveeswaram – Tamilnadu. He started an order – Theerthapatha.
His desciples – Neela kanda theertha pather & Parama hamsa theerrtha pather.
His vision of unity – Sama darsanam – it means experience unity in all. He said – Non Brahmins also have the right to learn Vedas and worship god.
Books – Punar janma niroopanam, Jeeva karunya niroopanam.

7. Poykayi Yohannan(Appachan) 1885-1939

Born in Paraya samudaya at Eraviperur, Pathanamthitta. Original name Komaran.

1909 – P.R.D.S. (Prathyaksha Reksha Daiva Sabha) – Then his name changed to Kumara Guru Devan.
1836 – Samathwa Samajam.
1900 – Ezhava Samajam – T.K. Madhavan.
1903 – SNDP – Current & Lifetime president – Sri Narayana Guru, Secretary – Kumaranasan, Vice president – Dr. Palpu. SNDP concept was from Dr. Palpu
1907 – Araya Samajam(Pandit Karuppan), Sadhu Jana Paripalana Sangam.
1908 – Nampoothiri Yoga Shema Sabha – VT Bhattathirippadu.
1909 – PRDS.
1914 – NSS(Nair Service Society) – 1st president – K. Kelappan, Gen-secretary - Mannathu Panmanabhan.
1917 – Sahodara Sangham – Cherai, Ernakulam.
1919 – Dharma Bhada Sangham.

Mannathu Panmanabhan

1914 – NSS.
1924 – Savarna Jatha.
1949 – Devaswom president.
1951 – Bharatha Kesari – given by Dr. Rajendra Prasad.
1959 – Vimochana Samaram.
1966 – Padma bhushan.
1989 – His stamp.

Dob – 1878 Jan 2.
Kerala nair samajam – 1907.
Nair samajam – 1912.
Nair brithya jana sangam – 1914.
1915 -  it renamed to NSS in the basis of servants of India society.
HQ – Penunna, Changanasseri.
First president – K. Kelappan, first general secretary – Mannathu padmanabhan.
1924 – Savarna jatha in the period of Vaikkom satyagraha.
Jeeva sika or deepa sika jatha – 1959.
1959 – He got title Bharatha kesari from Dr. Rajendra Prasad.
He known as Madan Mohan Malavya of Kerala.
