Third phase of Indian constitutional history – 1935-1946
1934 – the first individual who demanded Indian constituent assembly – MN. Roy.
1935 – the first political organization which demand CA – Swaraj Party.
1935 – INC working committee passed a resolution demanding the same.
1938 – Official declaration for CA by Nehru.
1940 – The offer which accepted Indian CA – August offer.

1942 – Cripps mission proposal for Indian CA. Gandhiji called it as post dated cheque drawn on a crimpling bank. He also declared Quit India as well as Do or Die.
1945 – Wavell plan and Simla conference for CA. But failed.

Last and final phase of Indian CA – 1946 -1950 Jan 26
1946 Mar 23 – Cabinet mission came to India. It includes 3 members – Pethwic Lawrence, Stafford Crips, AV. Alexander.
1946 May 16 – plan for CA elction. Classified India in to 5 categories:-
• A Group – Hindu majority members – 187.
• B Group – Muslim majority – 35.
• C Group – Others – 70.
Total – 292.
Governor provinces – 4.
Total – 296.
Actual participation of CA election – 296.
Princely states – 93.
Total – 389 – the total seats decided for CA election.

1946 Dec 6 – CA assembly came into being.
1946 Dec 9 – First meeting. Elected temporary president (Sachinanda Sinha). First person addressed CA – JB. Kripalani.
1946 Dec 11 – elected permanent president (Dr. Rajendraprasad). Vice president (HC. Mukharji). CA advisor (VN Rao).
1946 Dec 13 – objective resolution by Nehru.
1946 Dec 23 – Last day of 1st session.

1947 Jan 22 – acceptance of objective resolution as preample.
1947 Feb 20 – Clemend-Atlees declaration of withdrawal from India. 1947 Jan 3 – Mount Batten plan for partition.
1947 Jul 18 – Indian independence act passed.
1947 Jun 22 – National flag adopted.
1947 August 14 – CA converted to legislative assembly and received India.
1947 Aug 29 – appointed drafting committee. 7 members – Chairman – BR. Ambedkar.
First draft – 1948 Feb. Second draft – 1948 Oct. Final – 1948 Nov 4.

1949 Nov 26 – Indian CA accepted, adopted, enacted Indian constitution. Agreed by 284/299 members. Including 17 Keralites. 3 Malayali women – Anne Mascrene, Ammu Swaminathan, Dakshayani Velayudhan.
Total days for making Indian constitution – 2 years 11 months 18 days (aprox 1083 days).
1950 Jan 24 – Adopted national anthem, national song and elected president (Dr. Rajendraprasad).
1950 Jan 26 – Constitution came into being or came in to force or started functioning.
