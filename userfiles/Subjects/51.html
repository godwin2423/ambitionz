5.Cultural and educational right (29-30)

29 – to protect the language, culture and script of minority.
30 – to establish educational institutions for minorities.

32 – is known as – very heart and very soul of Indian constitution” – BR. Ambedkar. It is taken from Britain.

Prerogative ritt –it includes –

  1. Habeas corpus – it means to have the body off. It is issued when any person including public authority illegally detaining any other person.
  2. Mandamus – it means – we command. It is issued any when any public office or lower court not properly doing their duties.
  3. Prohibition – It means to forbid. It is issued when any public office or lower court exceed their jurisdiction.
  4. Certiorary – it means to to be certified or to be informed. It is issued for seeking information about case or transferring cases. Eg: Jayalalitha case.
  5. Quo warranto – it means by what authority or warrant. It is issued when any person illegally claiming any public office of permanent character.

Detailed info.

Article 14 exceptions
Art 361 – immunity for president and governor.
Art 194 – no court proceedings against MLAs for their speech in state legislative assembly.
Art 105 – no court proceedings against MPs for their speech in parliament.

Article 15 exceptions
1st amendment – 1951 – special provision for SC/ST and OBC.

Article 16 exceptions
49.5% minority reservation

Article 19
SAAMRP :-
19 (1) (a) – speech and expression – it includes freedom of press, right against telephone tapping, right to demonstrate freedom of silence.
19 (1) (b) – freedom of assembling.
19 (1) (c) – freedom of association.
19 (1) (d) – freedom of movement.
19 (1) (e) – freedom of residence.
19 (1) (g) – freedom of profession.

Article 21
It includes right to descent environment, right to health, right to privacy, right to information, right to livelihood, right to shelter.
