IV. Interrogative sentence.

A sentence ending with a question mark.

Direct question

Indirect question

Question word + be (is/are/ was/were) + subject + ?

Question word + subject + be + full stop

(Question word) + helping verb + subject + main verb + ?

(Question word) + subject + helping verb + main verb + full stop

 
1. Reporting verb should be ‘asked’.
2. No word is necessary to avoid inverted commas.
3. When there is no question word if or whether can be used.
4. Direct question form should be changed to indirect form.
5. First person and second person are changed to third person.
v1 -> v2.    V2 -> had v3.

“What is your name?” he said – He asked what his name was.
“Where is your car?” he said – He asked where his car was.
“Can I meet you?” he said – He asked if he could meet him.
“Are you going to the party?” he said – He asked whether he was going to the party.

Babu: “Raju, Raju, how often do you shave a day?”
Raju: “I shave 40 or 50 times a day”.
Babu: “What! Are you mad?”.
Raju: “I am not bad, I am a barber”.
Babu: “Have you completed your training?”.
Raju: “Yes, I have completed it, but I did not get a job”.
Babu: “Come to my office, I shall give you a job there.”

Babu asked Raju how often he shaved a day. Raju said that he shaved 40 or 50 times a day. Babu exclaimed and asked if he was mad. Raju said that he was not mad but he was a barber. Babu asked if he had completed his training. Raju said that he had completed it but he had not got a job. Babu asked him to go to his office and said that he would give him a job there.

Articles

Articles can be classified to 2. Definite article and indefinite article.

Definite article (The)

God, The gods, The Shiva, Man, The Alps, Everest, with Nature no need The.

‘The’ is used with the following:-
Rivers – The Ganga, The Godavari.
Sea – The Arabian sea.
Ocean -  The Atlantic ocean.
Government departments – The revenue department.
Holy books – The Bible, The Ramayana.
Name of trains – The Rajadhani express.
Bank – The SBI.
News paper – The Hindu.
The continent contains many groups – The USA.
When republic or kingdom comes – The USSR.
Superlative degrees – The tallest.
Desert – The sahara.
Ship – The Queen Victoria.
King – King Hentry The IV. (since there is ‘The’ before ‘IV’, it will not come before ‘King’ – one place only).
The cinema -
The bus stop –

I am going to college (going to college for study, so no need ‘The’).
I am going to hospital (going for treatment so no need The).
I am going to church.
I am going to the school to meet the class teacher of my son (not going for study).
I am going to the hospital to saw my friend.
I am going to the church to meet father.

University:- The + university + of + place name.
The university of Kerala.

From Kerala university:- a + university + in + place name.
A university in Kerala.

Normally ‘The’ won’t come with a preposition if it is before a noun.
By hand. By the hand – is wrong.

12 inch makes a foot. 24 inches make 2 feet.
